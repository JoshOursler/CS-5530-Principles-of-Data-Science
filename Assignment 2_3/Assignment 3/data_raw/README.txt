The raw_diabetes data contains data on 768 patients. The attributes recorded are
	Pregnancies (number of pregnancies the patient has had),
	Glucose (patient's glucose level measured in mg/dL),
	Blood Pressure (patient's blood pressure measured in mmHg),
	Skin Thickness (patient's skin thickness measured in mm),
	Insulin (patient's insluin level measured in pmol/L),
	BMI (patient's body mass index measured as lbs/in^2),
	Diabetes Pedigree Function (chance of having diabetes based on family history [0.078, 2.42] higher value indicates greater chance),
	Age (patient's age),
	Outcome (boolean as to whether the patient has diabetes or not [1] yes [0] no).
