The raw_train dataset contains information about cars that is used to determine the price of the used cars. The attributes recorded are:
	Make and Model of the car,
	Location or city of sale,
	Year of manufacture,
	Mileage which is the amount of miles per fuel the car gets and is recorded with different units (kmpl and km/kg),
	Odometer which is the number of kilometers the car has traveled,
	Fuel Type with either petrol, diesel, or electric,
	Transmission Type with either Manual or Automatic,
	Engine Displacement measured in CC,
	Engine Horsepower measured in bhp,
	Number of Seats,
	Price when the car was new measured in Lahks,
	and Price of the car as the output measured in Lahks.
